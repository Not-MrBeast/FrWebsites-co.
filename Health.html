Stevia: Health benefits, side effects and more

Stevia is a sugar substitute made from the leaves of the stevia plant and is classified as a non-nutritive substitute as it has zero to no calories per serving. It is made from a plant from the Asteraceae family. Reb-A is 200 to 300 times sweeter than table sugar. Sweeteners made from Reb-A are considered novel sweeteners as are a healthy alternative to artificial sweeteners as they are derived from natural sources.

you can add this stevia in your coffee, yougurt, tea, juices etc. This stevia is commonly used in many products in asia and south america.. Apart from the “zero calorie” name that stevia has acquired due to the low sucrose level, there are numerous health benefits linked with stevia.
